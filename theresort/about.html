ABOUT US

A Few Words About Us

When you are our guest, we welcome you into our family.

Here at the Resort we aim to deliver you a true Canadian cottage experience with all the luxuries of the city. From freshly prepared BBQ on our upper deck to exciting waterfront sports to enjoying a cold brewski on the dock. You should feel right at home.

Let us take care of you.

We have no less that four in-house guest rooms accommodating up to 15 people in comfortable top quality beds outfitted with European duvets and multiple pillows. For those discerning guests who require complete privacy we have the waterfront cabin or the loft in the woods, please note these option are not available in the winter season.

OUR ADVANTAGES

1.

Numerous Couch Options

There are three couches located on the main floor, all with a charming Canadian cottage plaid motif. There is also a sitting room looking out onto the lake in the lower floor in addition to a large sectional with movie theatre style setup.

2.

Fully Stocked Guest Amenities

The primary guest bathroom is fully stocked with everything you can need during your stay with us including towels, travel tooth brush sets, ear plugs, shampoo, conditioner, body wash, and a bevy of pharmaceutical products.

3.

Gym Facilities

As our guest you have 24 hour access private gym, with a stair master, treadmill and weight room setup. Cool off afterwards with a quick dip in the lake or relax in the soothing hot tub

4.

Happy Hour

The Resort is proud to host a daily hour with a variety of beverages available in the lower level drink fridge, all free-of-charge for our guests. Enjoy the setting sun from one of our waterfront docks, upper deck, or lounge by tiki bar.

5.

Chef Style Kitchen

Feel as if you are part of the action with our restaurant quality kitchen facilities, including everything a professional chef could require with stacked ovens, double door fridge, stainless steel dishwasher, and a stove mounted in the island for Top Chef style viewing.

6.

Pet Friendly

We are not only pet friendly, but encourage you to bring along your dog. Our retrieval specialist is especially welcoming and will be happy to walk you along the perimeter of the property

I solve complex problems by designing solutions

I'm a user interface specialist currently based in Montreal, Canada. Graduated as a System Design Engineer in 2014. Since then I have been working at Ciena on end-to-end custom solutions.

Now you’re probably wondering, “What is Systems Design?”, trust me you aren’t the first to have this question! I graduated as a Systems Design Engineer from the University of Waterloo in 2014. Systems Design takes an interdisciplinary approach to engineering problem solving combined with design project. Within my design groups I worked on a wide spectrum of projects ranging from an automated hospital triage system, self-heating gloves, and building furniture optimized for moving. In my final years, I specialized in interface and user experience design methodologies.

At Ciena, I work within the Services organization to develop custom web application for our clients. This covers the full end-to-end process including customer interactions, business to technical requirement translation, milestone definition, leading development cycles, and supporting deployment.

Supporting the developement, I have led the implementation of a sustainable agile process including playing the role of the scrum master. Our team sizes vary between 3-5 members on projects ranging from 6 months to a year. I complement this by leading the front-end developement as well as training new members of the team.

Aerial Photo

applied because I liked Art AttAck

While at university I was heavily involved in the organization of the engineering orientation week. I ran the Aerial Photo event from the last three years of my undergrad. This event involve creating a design, placement planning, and communicating for a successful event.

it's a journey

The design of the photo is based off the year, and the theme of that week. The photo is take off a building which mean it was take at an angle, as such clean straight lines are what makes a clear design. After the design is finalize we moved to an 'old school' method of transfer it to grid paper and scaling it.

The scaling on grid paper is to fit all the people in the photo with four people per metre squared. the final step is to divide the design by team, indicated by colour. Unfortunately that year it rained on the day of the event, which meant we moved to rain plan. This involved completly redoing the layout to match the small number of people.
